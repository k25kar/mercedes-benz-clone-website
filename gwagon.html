The interior of the G-Class.

The interior of the G-Class offers extensive highlights as standard including Widescreen Cockpit ambient lighting and Panoramic sunroof. Futuristic-looking display concept: two 12.3-inch screens for the instrument display and media display are installed together under a continuous glass cover. This way you can determine the content of the dynamic displays. With 3 display styles you can adjust how information is depicted in the instrument display to suit your preferences. The Panoramic sunroof allows more light and air into your vehicle. Even when closed, and especially when open, it lends a brighter ambience to the interior. Showcase the interior according to your personal preferences or current mood. The 64 colours of the ambient lighting create exciting colour themes which can alternate dynamically or illuminate several areas in different colours. Enjoy sophisticated ease of use and the easy access to the multimedia functions: finger gestures (multi-touch) make the touchpad as intuitive to operate as a smartphone. In addition, the familiar Controller and direct-access keys are available for frequently used functions. With the infinitely electrically adjustable front seats, you and your front passenger can always find a comfortable, back-friendly position.
