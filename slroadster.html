Highlights of the Mercedes-AMG SL Roadster exterior.

The Mercedes-AMG SL, a new edition of an automotive icon, returns to its roots with a classic soft top and powerful, dynamic character. The AMG-specific radiator grille characterises the powerful front end, while the DIGITAL LIGHT LED headlamps lend additional presence. The flared wheel arches in combination with the AMG light-alloy wheels lend a perfect balance of elegance and sportiness. Flowing surfaces and recessed door handles emphasise the purist design appeal. The wide track width and rear curvature emphasise the width, while the narrow LED rear lights accentuate the rear view.






Highlights of the Mercedes-AMG SL Roadster interior.

The best outlooks for sporty driving pleasure and a superior driving experience: the head-up display transforms your windscreen into an exciting digital cockpit. This means you always have a direct view of key information. Your full attention remains on the road and the traffic conditions ahead of you. Not available on AMG SL 43 Premium or AMG SL 43 Touring. To help you find your way in complex traffic situations, MBUX Augmented Reality for Navigation networks the virtual world with reality. The technology incorporates graphic navigation instructions and traffic information in real images. You can reach your destination quickly, safely and stress-free. Not available on AMG SL 43 Premium or AMG SL 43 Touring. The free-standing central display on the centre console is the dominant control surface for navigation, MBUX functions or automatic climate control. To avoid disruptive light reflections caused by different sun positions when the fabric top is open, the inclination of the touch screen can be electrically adjusted from 12° to 32°. The ENERGIZING packages contribute to your support through comfort programmes. Ten-minute programs direct air conditioning, ambient lighting, music and seat functions such as massage or heating/ventilation. You will be amazed at how your concentration can be improved on long journeys. Not available on AMG SL 43 Premium or AMG SL 43 Touring. Discover the advantages of the intelligent MBUX voice assistant - no fixed commands are necessary. With the keyword "Hey Mercedes" you activate the voice input and can add a specific operating request, for example for navigation, automatic climate control or changing the radio station. The sound of a luxurious home entertainment system, optimised for the interior of your Mercedes-AMG. Innovative actuator technology creates three-dimensional sound via the roof liner. With the VIP settings, you can find the perfect listening position as if you were in your own living room, and choose different sound patterns - entirely according to your own taste and music style. On the high-resolution driver display, you always have all the important information in view. The display in the instrument cluster can be personalised with different display styles, AMG-specific displays and individually selectable main views. The display is easily visible through a visor, even when the sun is shining and the soft top is open.





Double Impact. SL 43 & SL 55

With the optional rear-axle steering with a steering angle of up to 2.5°, you can drive more agilely through bends and experience even more driving pleasure. Depending on the vehicle's speed, you can enjoy particularly agile and spontaneous steering characteristics like in a vehicle with a short wheelbase, It gives stability during dynamic rides and supports performance in every way. From comfortable cruising to a sporty driving style: the suspension adapts to your wishes at the push of a button. With COMFORT you get balanced damping characteristics, while SPORT and SPORT+ support you with stiffer tuning when you adopt a sporty driving style – the choice is yours! The five AMG DYNAMIC SELECT driving programs, as well as the "RACE" included in the optional AMG DYNAMIC PLUS package, enable a wide range of vehicle characteristics from comfortable to dynamic. The individual driving programs offer an individual driving experience, precisely adapted to different driving conditions. The AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9G transmission provides optimum support for a dynamic driving style and brings genuine motorsport flair to every journey. Sporty, fast gear changes or comfortable, barely noticeable shifts depending on the driving mode: thanks to the nine gears, the engine is often in the optimal rev range.





SL 63 4MATIC+

The further-developed 4.0-litre V8 engine from Mercedes-AMG impresses in both output levels with its explosive power delivery combined with finely-adjustable output. Numerous high-tech components and materials from motor racing are used. The twin-scroll turbochargers are placed between the cylinder heads ("hot inner V") for spontaneous response and low exhaust emissions. Cylinder shutoff when power demand is low reduces fuel consumption. For the first time, the SL delivers its power to the road with the state-of-the-art AMG Performance 4MATIC+ all-wheel drive system. The all-wheel drive variably links the rear axle to the front axle and thus guarantees ideal torque distribution. The spontaneous responsiveness is in part thanks to the AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9G transmission. Relaxed cruising with smooth gearshifts optimised for efficiency and comfort. In the "Sport" and "Sport+" driving modes, correspondingly uncompromising and with extremely short gear changes in line with the driving situation. The innovative AMG ACTIVE RIDE CONTROL suspension with active roll stabilisation using intelligent hydraulics enables improved turn-in and load change behaviour with typical AMG dynamics, precision and feedback for the driver. At the same time, it increases ride comfort when driving straight ahead and driving over bumps. The six AMG DYNAMIC SELECT driving programs "Smooth", "Comfort", "Sport", "Sport +", "Individual" and "RACE" enable a range of vehicle characteristics from comfortable to dynamic. Each driving program offers an individual driving experience with precise tuning for a wide range of driving conditions. Rear-axle steering with a steering angle of up to 2.5° increases agility and driving pleasure, especially when cornering quickly. Up to 100 km/h, the rear axle steers in the opposite direction and virtually shortens the wheelbase. From 100 km/h, it then steers in the same direction and thus virtually extends the wheelbase, which supports driving safety.
